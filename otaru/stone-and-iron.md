# Stone and Iron (石と鉄 STONE and IRON)

- **Type:** Cafe / Western Dining
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/nB6m7SYo1sXxQMpo6
- **Note:** All prices are tax-included. Rice is Niseko-produced Kamui blend. Lunch 11:30–15:00, Dinner from 17:00. Single-item lunch orders require 1 drink order (sets are better value). Children 5 and under are exempt. Set drink can be upgraded to alcohol (draft beer, Hokkaido wine white or red) for +150 yen. Galettes made with 100% Rankoshi buckwheat flour and Kuromatsunai mineral water.

# Hashibirokou (ハシビロコウ)

- **Type:** Cafe (Morning / Lunch / Sweets)
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/aNU7R8NZTncNyKfD9
- **Note:** AM 7:00–11:00 is morning set only. Hot coffee during morning is morning blend only; refills are charged. Water is self-service until 11:00 AM. Items marked with a red dot include a freshly baked Belgian waffle. +300 yen for a drink set (waffle not included; mix juice is 400 yen). Snacks (おつまみ) included with liquor; without snacks save 150 yen.

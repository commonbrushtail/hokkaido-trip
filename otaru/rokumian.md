# Rokumian (食堂 六味庵 / ろくみあん)

- **Type:** Korean-Japanese Donburi (Samgyeopsal Rice Bowls)
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/RtkU4TzhhiTGKHKN9
- **Hours:** 11:00 AM – 7:00 PM, closed Sundays
- **Phone:** 0134-33-0955
- **Address:** Hokkaido Otaru-shi Sumiyoshi-cho 8-18
- **Note:** All bowls come with pickles and miso soup. Special sizes have 1.5x meat and rice. The special is a service item — please share if ordering for big eaters. Takeout available at same price (container included).

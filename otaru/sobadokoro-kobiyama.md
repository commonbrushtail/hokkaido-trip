# Sobadokoro Kobiyama (そば処 こびやま)

- **Type:** Soba (Buckwheat Noodles)
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/6HFPPg4F3mo8fXRY6
- **Note:** All prices are tax-included. 冷 = cold, 温 = hot. Two soba types available: 100% buckwheat (十割そば 800 yen) and thick black soba (黒太そば 1,000 yen), both available cold or hot.

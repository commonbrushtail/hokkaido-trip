# Broiler Garden Ookubo (ブロイラーガーデン オオクボ)

- **Type:** Charcoal Grill (炭火焼き) — Chicken, Yakiniku, Jingisukan
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/HuZ5i38L4TA8CEBH6
- **Hours:** Weekends & holidays 12:00 ~ 20:00 (phone reservation preferred); Weekdays by phone reservation only (平日は電話予約のみ)
- **Season:** Early April ~ End of November
- **Note:** Order at the counter. Please return dishes to the counter after eating. Soup, tea, seasonal fruit are complimentary. Vegetables served with orders (availability varies). Garlic and lemon juice available on request.

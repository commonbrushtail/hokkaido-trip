# Nahana (菜はな)

- **Type:** Cafe / Lunch Set Meals (Hokkaido ingredients focus)
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/XpdCsHUndKctRMwDA
- **Hours:** 11:00–16:00 (L.O. 15:00), closed Sundays & holidays, all seats non-smoking
- **Note:** Photos are examples; salads, side dishes, and extras vary by season and day. Items with the Kyoya Seimen owl logo use noodles/gyoza skins custom-made by Otaru's popular ramen shop Kyoya (京や) / Kyoya Seimen (京や製麺). Eggs are soft-cooked with butter by default — ask staff to change. Rice choice: white or brown rice. +210 yen to upgrade rice to fluffy whipped egg on rice (ふわとろ卵かけご飯).

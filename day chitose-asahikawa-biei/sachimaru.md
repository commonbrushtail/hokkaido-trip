# Sachimaru (さちまる)

- **Type:** Teishoku / Set Meals (Fried Chicken & Braised Pork Specialty)
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/yFu3wDhjN1WpLyys5
- **Note:** All prices include tax. Portions are large — you can request less rice (ご飯少なめ, ご飯半分) when ordering. Cold water refills are self-service near the entrance. Takeout containers available (30 yen).

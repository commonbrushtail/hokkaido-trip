# パニエ 富良野 (Panier Furano)

- **Type:** Black Curry, Yoshoku (Western-style Japanese), Gratin, Doria, Pizza, Ramen
- **Address:** 北海道富良野市山部中町2-61 (Furano-shi, Yamabe Nakamachi 2-61)
- **Phone:** 0167-56-7727
- **Hours:** 11:30-15:00, 18:00-21:00 (LO 20:30)
- **Closed:** Irregular holidays
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/yHBFfj7eyjZZX6b67
- **Website:** https://panier-restaurant.com/
- **Note:** Cash/yen only. Tax 10% included. Order by number. All curry is spicy black curry served with rice and salad. Large curry +200 yen. Large ramen +100 yen. Nabeyaki udon available Nov-Mar only. Relocated from Kitanomine to Yamabe in 2020.

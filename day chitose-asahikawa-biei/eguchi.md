# 焼肉の店 えぐち (Yakiniku no Mise Eguchi)

- **Type:** Yakiniku (Japanese BBQ)
- **Address:** 北海道旭川市南一条通26 (Asahikawa-shi, Minami 1-jo Dori 26)
- **Phone:** 0166-33-2550
- **Hours:** 16:00-22:00 (LO food & alcohol 21:30)
- **Closed:** Every Tuesday, 2nd Monday of each month
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/CWr96b74ZTLtTkTg7
- **Note:** Cash/yen only. 28 seats (counter 6 + tables). Parking 3-4 cars. Non-smoking. Reservations accepted. Order by number. All meat portions are 100g each.

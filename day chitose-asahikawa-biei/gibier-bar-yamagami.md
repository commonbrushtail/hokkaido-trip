# ジビエバル 山神 (Gibier Bar Yamagami)

- **Type:** Gibier (Game Meat) Bar, Curry, Izakaya
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/LsW2ZDu4mcpbGzzz5
- **Note:** Specializes in Hokkaido game meats — Ezo deer, boar, and bear. Lunch is curry only. Dinner has full menu with game meat dishes, tempura vegetables, and seasonal specials. "We heat slowly, so it takes a bit of time. We serve you the best plate."

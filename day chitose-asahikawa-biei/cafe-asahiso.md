# Cafe Asahiso (cafe 旭荘)

- **Type:** Coffee & Pizza Cafe
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/hUgP3knVxFGK8Eat6
- **Hours:** Tue-Sat 10:30-17:00
- **Phone:** 0166-52-0031

*Uses Hokkaido Biei wheat and Tokachi cheese for pizza. Flour milled by Ebetsu Seifun. Soft cream made with fresh organic raw milk from North Plain Farm in Okoppe — no food additives. All prices include tax. Soft cream, cakes, and parfaits also available for takeout.*

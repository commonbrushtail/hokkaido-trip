# 焼鳥らんまん (Yakitori Ranman)

- **Type:** Yakitori, Shinko-yaki (Asahikawa specialty)
- **Address:** 北海道旭川市五条通7 (Asahikawa-shi, Gojo-dori 7)
- **Phone:** 0166-23-7712
- **Hours:** 17:00–21:30 (LO 21:00)
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/9fCxx92LcJ9QpWuPA
- **Note:** Asahikawa's famous Shinko-yaki specialty. Founded in the Showa era. Motto: "Once you eat it, you can't stop! Drink, eat, laugh."

---

# Yukito Nikuto (肉ビストロ ユキトニクト)

- **Type:** Meat Bistro / Crystal Plate Yakiniku
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/YZQmoFm5pYRV6CH56
- **Location:** Asahikawa (旭川市3条通6丁目右10号 ステップファーストビル地下)
- **Hours:** 17:00–24:00
- **Note:** All prices include tax. Speciality is "Kobu-Kuro" — rare Hokkaido kuroge wagyu raised on Hidaka kelp at Matsumoto Ranch (Hidaka, Misawa-cho). Crystal plate grilling produces minimal oil/smoke, no smell on clothes, and far-infrared for juicy results. Rice is pesticide-free Yumepirika from Beaver Farm Kitagawa in Kamifurano. Some items are limited quantity (数量限定).

# 蕎麦と料理 おかだ紅雪庭 (Soba to Ryouri Okada Kousetsutei)

- **Type:** Soba, Japanese Cuisine, Course Meals
- **Phone:** 0166-22-5570
- **Hours:** Lunch 11:30~15:00 (LO 14:30), Dinner 17:30~21:30 (LO 21:00)
- **Closed:** Sundays — reservations must be made by the previous day at 15:00; Sunday reservations require booking by Friday 15:00 (market closed)
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/gyimuzPazhdXLRoR9
- **Note:** All prices include tax (内税). 1F: counter seats ~20, soba, sake, daily specials, a la carte. 2F: private rooms (kaiseki), up to 8 guests per room, larger groups can use the main hall. Lunch private rooms also available upon request. Available for gatherings, weddings, memorial services — inquire for details. Free rice service available upon request.

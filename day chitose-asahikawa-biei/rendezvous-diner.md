# Rendezvous Diner (ランデブーダイナー)

- **Type:** Yoshoku Diner (Curry, Spaghetti, Omurice, Plate Lunch)
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/1LtFwnYE5VH5iATE7
- **Note:** Choose bread or rice with plate lunches. Lunch plates also available in the evening. Toppings sold separately and can be added to any dish. Takeout available. Spice curry comes in medium-spicy and mild (2 types); toppings are mix-and-match.

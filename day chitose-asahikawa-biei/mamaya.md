# 釜めし ままや (Kamameshi Mamaya)

- **Type:** Kamameshi (Clay Pot Rice), Set Meals, Izakaya
- **Address:** 北海道空知郡奈井江町字奈井江本町6区 (Naie-cho, Sorachi-gun)
- **Phone:** 0125-65-5560
- **Hours:** Lunch 11:30–14:00, Dinner 17:00–20:00 (LO 19:30)
- **Closed:** Every Wednesday
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/9NxL5nLDvo6zVGZV8
- **Note:** 294m from Naie Station. 17 seats (counter 5 + raised seating + 2F tatami). Parking ~4 cars. Cash only. Non-smoking. Reservations recommended. Kamameshi can be prepared to your arrival time if you call ahead. Takeout available. Family-friendly with raised seating areas.

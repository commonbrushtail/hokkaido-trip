# 豚丼 まぜそば Moro (Butadon and Mazesoba Moro)

- **Type:** Butadon (豚丼 / Pork Rice Bowl) and Mazesoba (まぜそば / Mixed Noodles) - Japanese
- **Address:** 北海道夕張郡栗山町中央2-73 ハイム西山 1F (Kuriyama-cho, Yubari-gun)
- **Note:** Restaurant may have relocated to 栗山町錦 (Kuriyama-cho Nishiki) as of Aug 2025. Verify before visiting.
- **Hours:**
  - Lunch: 11:00 to 14:00
  - Dinner: 17:00 to 20:00
- **Closed:** Wednesday, 1st Sunday
- **Parking:** Nearby shopping street parking (50m)
- **Payment:** Cash, electronic money, PayPay
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/k8F4nzT1zYdFuffy9

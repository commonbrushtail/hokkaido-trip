# カフェスタンド プロペラ (Cafe Stand Propeller)

- **Type:** Cafe - Curry, Banh Mi, Pancakes, Coffee
- **Address:** 北海道岩見沢市7条西21丁目2-3 (Iwamizawa-shi, 7-jo Nishi 21-2-3)
- **Phone:** 0126-35-7337
- **Hours:** 8:00 to 17:00 (LO 16:30)
- **Closed:** Every Wednesday
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/1xXS5jb5W7aMKxfU7

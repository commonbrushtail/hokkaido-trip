# THE LOCAL

- **Type:** Cafe, Specialty Coffee, Desserts
- **Address:** 6-21-320, 2JYO-DORI, Asahikawa City (旭川市二条通)
- **Hours:** 10:00~17:00
- **Established:** 2023
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/TF97WvuTiWEHQt6e6
- **Note:** Sugar-free desserts (sweetened with honey only). Uses VOREAS acacia honey. Allergen icons on menu (wheat, dairy, egg). Plant-based options available. Customize milk to almond milk (+20 yen).

## Coffee

Roasted by nido. "RAWLAW blend" (organic JAS certified) — a blend of lightly acidic Ethiopian Sidamo (light roast) and slightly bitter Guatemalan City roast (deep roast).

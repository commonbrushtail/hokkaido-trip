# 飯や 山親爺 (Meshiya Yamaoyaji)

- **Type:** Japanese Set Meals (Teishoku), Casual Dining
- **Address:** 北海道岩見沢市栄町1-20-1 (Iwamizawa-shi, Sakaemachi 1-20-1)
- **Hours:** 11:30–13:30 (weekdays only)
- **Closed:** Saturday, Sunday
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/x29JYCVDyBkCSvfb9
- **Note:** Opened December 2024. 18 seats (counter + tables). Parking available. Credit cards, electronic money, QR codes accepted. Takeout available for all menu items — ask staff. Extra rice +100 yen.

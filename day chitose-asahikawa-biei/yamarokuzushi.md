# やま六鮨 (Yama Roku Zushi)

- **Type:** Sushi, Seafood Bowl (Kaisen-don)
- **Address:** 北海道江別市二条3-6-5 (Ebetsu-shi, Nijo 3-6-5)
- **Phone:** 011-384-2525
- **Hours:** 11:00–14:00, 17:00–22:00 (LO 21:30)
- **Closed:** Every Monday
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/r7P3nRtFu3hZRMZbA
- **Website:** https://www.yamarokuzushi.com/
- **Note:** Founded in Showa 2 (1927), nearly 100 years old. Reservations accepted. Private rooms available. Parking available. Credit cards and electronic money accepted. All prices are tax-included. +200 yen for coffee with lunch.

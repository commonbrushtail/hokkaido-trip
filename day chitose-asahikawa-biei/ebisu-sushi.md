# えびす寿司 (Ebisu Sushi)

- **Type:** Sushi, Sashimi, Maki Rolls
- **Address:** 旭川市四条通十六丁目 (Asahikawa-shi, Yojo-dori 16-chome)
- **Hours:** 16:00~
- **Closed:** Every Monday (毎週月曜日)
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/UEmMesNciZfLuZHM8
- **Note:** Uses domestic rice (国内産米). Otoshi (table charge appetizer) 500 yen. Fresh seasonal toppings prepared daily.

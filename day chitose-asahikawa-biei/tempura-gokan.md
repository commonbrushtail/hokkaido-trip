# Tempura Gokan (てんぷら 五感)

- **Type:** High-End Tempura (Counter Omakase)
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/29xU7LgKbCdJTPQP6
- **Note:** All prices include tax (税込). Omakase courses are counter-seat only and require reservation 3 days in advance. Cancellation policy: 50% the day before, 100% same day. Changes/cancellations accepted up to 2 days prior. Seasonal local sake, rare Hokkaido wines, Japanese whiskey, and luxury beers paired with tempura are available.

# 鮨処 幸寿司 (Ko Sushi)

- **Type:** Sushi (寿司) - Japanese Seafood
- **Address:** 〒061-1352 北海道恵庭市島松仲町1丁目6番13号
- **Phone:** 0123-36-8717
- **Hours:**
  - Lunch: 11:30 to 14:00 (LO 13:45)
  - Dinner: 17:00 to 22:00
- **Closed:** Every Monday
- **Access:** About 3 min walk from JR Shimamatsu Station
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/RRhh9PtWVUSrMmMB7
- **Note:** The signature Kosushi-don (2,200 yen) is limited to 30 per day. People line up before opening even on weekdays.

# 和食や 花あかり (Washokuya Hanaakari)

- **Type:** Udon, Tempura, Japanese Set Meals
- **Address:** 北海道江別市向ケ丘14-3 (Ebetsu-shi, Mukougaoka 14-3)
- **Phone:** 011-389-8030
- **Hours:** 11:00–14:00, 17:00–
- **Closed:** Every Monday
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/oPTRdUQVQoWDF6qf6
- **Website:** https://hana-akari.info/
- **Note:** Cash only. No reservations. 35 seats (counter 7 + tables + tatami). Parking 13 spaces. Kid-friendly with children's chairs. Wheelchair/stroller accessible ramp. Self-made udon using 100% Ebetsu wheat. Tempura fried fresh to order. Also known for original baumkuchen.

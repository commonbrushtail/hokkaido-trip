**Soup Large Serving:** +500 yen

#### Shrimp Broth Intensity (海老ダシの濃さ)

Choose from 5 levels:
1. Niccori (にっこり) — Light
2. Tappuri (たっぷり) — Moderate
3. Gassuri (がっつり) — Strong
4. Kottori (こってり) — Rich
5. Bikkuri (びっくり) — Maximum

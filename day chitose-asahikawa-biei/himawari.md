# お食事処 向日葵 (Oshokujidokoro Himawari)

- **Type:** Handmade Soba, Set Meals, Izakaya (evening)
- **Address:** 〒078-2512 北海道雨竜郡北竜町和7-6 (Hokuryu-cho, Uryu-gun — next to Hokuren gas station)
- **Phone:** 0164-34-6009
- **Hours:** Lunch 11:30–14:00 (LO 13:30), Dinner 17:00–20:00 (LO 19:30)
- **Closed:** Every Sunday, 2nd & 4th Thursday
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/jfhNnkLstNvtLWgc8
- **Website:** https://himawari-hokuryu.com/
- **Instagram:** https://www.instagram.com/himawari_hokuryu/
- **Note:** Opened May 2018. All soba is handmade. Can substitute udon for soba. Extra soba +200 yen. Rice: small 100 yen, large 200 yen. Lunch drinks 250 yen. Uses local Hokuryu ingredients. Ramen is discontinued (終了).

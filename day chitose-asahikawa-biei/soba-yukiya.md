# 蕎麦 雪屋 (Soba Yukiya)

- **Type:** Soba (Buckwheat Noodles)
- **Google Maps:** https://maps.app.goo.gl/SF8VxxrYAdmGaKnp9
- **Hours:** Lunch 11:30–14:00 / Dinner 18:00–24:00 (Closed Sundays & holidays)
- **Note:** All prices include tax. Uses 100% Hokkaido buckwheat (十割蕎麦) from Kamikawa Town "Kitawase" variety. No wheat flour used — tempura is fried in buckwheat flour. Cold soba recommended. Large serving +200 yen. Additional dipping sauce +100 yen. Vegan/Halal items marked on menu.
